How to Engineer a Good Day

You know those people who always seem to be having a great day? The ones you’ve never seen upset, stressed, or tired. People whose brains seem like they’re powered by an infinite supply of sunshine, rainbows, sparkles, and glitter.

I’ve always wondered how they do it. Even though I realize that always being that happy is an unrealistic goal, I’ve found a few strategies that consistently work to optimize for a good day.

The secret? Priming your brain in the morning.

Here's my running list:

  • Get a good night’s sleep (very important).
  • Wake up and listen to “Another Day Of Sun.” Game-changing.
  • Positive mindset (most important):
    • Think about everything you’re grateful for.
    • Know there’s no problem you can’t get through.
    • Remember this story.
    • Advice Aloe Blacc shared with me: Don't sweat the small stuff.
  • Exercise and/or meditate.
  • Cold shower.
  • Stay hydrated.
  • If all else fails: take vitamin B.
