3D Printer Filament Extruder

Functionality:

  • Waste plastic is placed in the funnel, which uses a fan to push the bits downward in a zero-gravity environment
  • Plastic falls into crevasses in an spinning auger drill bit, pushing it forward in the pipe toward the band heater
  • Voltage readings from a thermocouple are converted into temperature, then used to regulate the heater as needed
  • Melted plastic is pushed out of a nozzle, yielding plastic filament

Result:

Our device was featured in CBS News and placed 2nd nationally in a NASA TI contest.
