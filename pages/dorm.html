The Ultimate Dorm Welcoming System

Functionality:

  • Motion sensor detects person approaching the door, resulting in a warm, British welcome from the system (“Greetings, please enter the passcode for room 216, Kingdom of Rana and Misikir”)
  • User must perform the appropriate hand motion to enter (place hand close to the sensor, then 2 feet away, then close to the sensor).
  • The correct password results in a motor turning 180 degrees, pulling down a rope connected to the locked door’s handle
  • Once the door is pushed open, another motion sensor activates speakers to play welcoming music
  • LED lights blink to the beat of the song using a sound sensor
  • Caveat: Due to the fact that we couldn't use screws to hold parts in place against the door (unless we wanted a huge fine), we resorted to using an ungodly amount of duct tape

Built with Ion, Jay, and Molly for our Things About Stuff final. Watch our promotional video below.
